About Us

History


SaffCoZ was founded in 2010 and has since become a well-known perfume brand for its exquisite perfumes that combine high-quality components with creative smell characteristics. The business began with a limited range of scents that rapidly became famous owing to its distinctive and long-lasting properties.

By 2015, SaffCoZ had expanded its product line to include a wide selection of perfumes that appealed to a variety of tastes, from sweet and fruity scents to deep, musky, and powerful scents. SaffCoZ's commitment to quality was not limited to its product offerings. The firm invested significantly in R&D to guarantee that each scent was made with the best components, drawing influence from both new trends and timeless classics.

Vision


To become the world's beloved and sought-after luxury perfume brand, creating timeless smells that captivate the senses and elicit memorable emotions while always raising the bar for quality, creativity, and customer experience in the fragrance business.

Mission


Crafting Excellence: To create perfumes using only the finest ingredients, combining artisanal craftsmanship with innovative techniques to deliver memorable, long-lasting scents that resonate with our customers.

Global Reach: To expand our brand's presence across the world, reaching new markets and making our signature perfumes accessible to a global audience through a seamless shopping experience.

Number of Perfumes Released


Since its start in 2010, SaffCoZ has created 24 distinct scents. These comprise a diverse spectrum of perfumes, from sweet and fruity to musky and bold, appealing to a variety of tastes and styles. Each perfume is made from high-quality components, demonstrating the brand's dedication to luxury and long-lasting smell characteristics.
